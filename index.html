About BURGER_AKA

Great Food Comes First
Every day, more than 2 million guests visit BURGER_AKA around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 1900 , BURGER_AKA is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in the world. The original Home of the Whopper, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 50 successful years.
